Genvium

Genvium is our flagship desktop platform designed to help startups identify, organize, and explore funding opportunities through a centralized searchable database.

The platform brings together grants, tax credits, startup incentive programs, and other funding resources into one streamlined experience, helping founders spend less time researching and more time executing.

During its first month after launch, Genvium welcomed over 15 early users — an encouraging start as we continue improving the platform and expanding available funding resources.
